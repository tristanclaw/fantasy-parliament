# Fantasy Parliament League Rules

Welcome to the Fantasy Parliament League! Manage your own roster of Canadian Members of Parliament and compete for the top of the leaderboard based on real-world political activity.

## 1. Roster Management
- **Team Captain:** Your primary MP. Typically represents your home riding.
- **Team Members:** You can draft up to 4 additional MPs to your roster.
- **Local-First:** Your team is stored in your browser's local storage. No accounts required.

## 2. Scoring System
MPs earn points based on their activity in the House of Commons:
- **Speeches:** Earn points for every speech or intervention in the House.
- **Votes:** Earn points for participating in official votes.
- **Sponsorships:** Earn points for sponsoring or supporting bills.

*Note: Data is synchronized daily from OpenParliament.ca.*

## 3. Data Retention
- The league tracks performance on a **rolling 7-day window**.
- Older data is pruned to keep the league fresh and the database efficient.

## 4. Leaderboard
- Submit your team's total score to the global anonymous leaderboard.
- Compete against other "Political Junkies" across Canada!
